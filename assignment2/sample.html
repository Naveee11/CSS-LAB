About M&M's

M&M's are colorful button-shaped chocolates, each of which has the letter "M" printed on one side, consisting of a candy shell surrounding a filling which varies depending upon the variety of M&M's.

Our Flavors

  • Milk Chocolate
  • Peanut
  • Crispy
  • Peanut Butter
  • Pretzel
